About Us

About GEMS EV

Established in 2024, GEMSev Mobility is a Chennai-based company at the forefront of the electric automotive revolution in India. Headquartered in the thriving EV hub of Chennai, we are dedicated to designing, manufacturing, and delivering a wide range of electric vehicles that cater to diverse passenger travel, commercial movement, and individual transportation needs. Our leadership team comprises seasoned entrepreneurs and experienced automotive professionals with a deep understanding of the Indian market. We have partnered withleading automotive design and manufacturing companies to leverage their expertise and deliver high-quality products.
